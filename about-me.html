WHO AM I?

Hey, I'm Ayoub. I was born in Al Hoceima, Morocco, grew up in Rabat, and now I live in Montreal. Telling my own story has never been easy, but if there's one thing that's been a constant in my life, it's soccer.

It all started when I was a kid—playing in the streets, watching games with family, arguing about teams with friends, and even spending hours on soccer video games. What began as fun slowly became a real passion, one that's been with me through every stage of my life.

Today, I carry that love proudly. I support my hometown club, CRA (Chabab Rif Al Hoceima), while also following the ups and downs of OM and Arsenal. For me, soccer isn't just a sport—it's a way of connecting, learning, and feeling part of something bigger.

WHY THIS BLOG?

This blog is my way of sharing that passion with you. Whether it's breaking down the latest match, discussing transfer rumors, or reminiscing about classic moments, I want to create a space where football fans can connect, learn, and enjoy the beautiful game together.
